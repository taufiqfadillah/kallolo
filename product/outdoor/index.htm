Summer feeling on the terrace - ceramic tiles for indoors and outdoors

Robust tiles for summer terrace enjoyment

If you want to spend the summer in your own garden or on your patio, ceramic tiles are the ideal choice. These tiles are particularly robust and resistant to various weather conditions such as heat and frost. They are also resistant to acids, grease and dirt and offer the same ease of care as indoor tiles. Thanks to the glaze, they hardly absorb any liquid, which makes them particularly easy to clean and durable - perfect for a beautiful and functional patio area.

Interior and exterior in harmony - creating a generous sense of space

For homes with floor-to-ceiling windows, ceramic tiles offer an exciting design option: The same tiles can be laid inside and outside, creating a seamless transition between the living room and terrace. This visual extension allows the indoor and outdoor areas to merge harmoniously and gives the entire living space a more spacious, open ambience. The result is a continuous design that is both functional and aesthetically pleasing and makes summer at home even more enjoyable.
